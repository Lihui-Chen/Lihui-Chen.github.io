I am an assistant researcher at Chongqing University, China. I received the B.Sc. degree in electronics and information engineering and the Ph.D. degree in information and communication engineering from Sichuan University, Chengdu, Sichuan, China, in 2018 and 2022. He visited the National Research Council, Institute of Methodologies for Environmental Analysis, CNR-IMAA, Italy, from Aug. 2021 to Aug. 2022. I am interested in image restoration, remote sensing image fusion and analysis, machine learning for image processing and computer vision, and visual language foundation for remote sensing.
